004 : Quick-Start Apps



1. Use the combo [window key] + [1] to [9] to activate or minimize the corisponding programs from your taskbar.


2. Use the numeric keys on the top of the keyboard.


3. As you can see in this small demo, while the mouse is in the middle of the screen, pressing win+2 windows explorer starts which is the 2nd icon. By pressing win+2 again it gets minimized.


If these steps are followed carefully...it works perfectly! I use it at work. Contact me if there is any problem.
